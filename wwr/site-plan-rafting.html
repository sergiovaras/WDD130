Overview

Purpose

Rafting is an exciting water activity that involves descending whitewater rivers in an inflatable raft. In short, rafting is an exciting activity that combines nature, exercise and teamwork. Dare to try it!

Audience

Discover the Thrill of Rafting in Nature! What is Rafting? Rafting is an aquatic adventure that immerses you in the beauty of the rivers and the excitement of the rapids. Imagine being on an inflatable raft, facing waves and currents while working as a team with your fellow travelers. It is an unforgettable experience! What benefits of Rafting? Exercise and Fun: Paddling rafting is an excellent cardiovascular exercise. You work your muscles while having fun in the water. As practiced? Equipment: We use inflatable rafts and life jackets. The guide will give you instructions so that you can enjoy it to the fullest. Rowing Technique: Row following the guide's instructions. Is easier than it looks like!
